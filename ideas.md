## Idea 1: "Midnight Counsel" — Dark Professional Authority

**Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalist meets Corporate Midnight

**Core Principles:**
- Deep authority through darkness — navy/slate backgrounds signal gravitas and trust
- Emerald green as the single accent — evokes notarial stamps, legal authority, and reliability
- Asymmetric content blocks — break the grid to feel crafted, not templated
- Glassmorphism cards — frosted glass over dark gradients for depth

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: `#0B1120` (deep midnight navy) — conveys authority and professionalism
- Surface: `#111827` (dark slate) — layered depth
- Accent: `#10B981` (emerald) — the color of notary stamps, trust, and action
- Text: `#F9FAFB` / `#D1D5DB` — clean legibility on dark
- Emotional intent: "We are the trusted authority in the room."

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed hero with diagonal clip-path cut at bottom
- Sticky glassmorphism navbar with blur backdrop
- Two-column asymmetric service cards (60/40 split)
- Staggered entrance animations on scroll

**Signature Elements:**
- Emerald left-border accent lines on section headers
- Glassmorphism cards with `backdrop-blur` and subtle border glow
- Diagonal section dividers between content blocks

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states lift cards with emerald glow shadow
- CTA buttons pulse subtly to draw attention
- Mobile menu slides in from right with overlay

**Animation:**
- Fade-up on scroll for all content blocks (framer-motion)
- Staggered children animations in service lists
- Smooth scroll behavior site-wide

**Typography System:**
- Headers: Plus Jakarta Sans 700/800 — bold, modern, authoritative
- Body: Outfit 400/500 — clean, readable, professional
- Accent labels: Outfit 600 uppercase tracking-widest

## Idea 2: "Legal Ivory" — Light Minimalist Trust

**Design Movement:** Swiss Minimalism + Legal Document Aesthetic

**Core Principles:**
- Ivory/cream backgrounds evoking paper documents
- Thin serif typography for headers, sans-serif body
- Extreme whitespace as the primary design element
- Subtle watermark patterns in backgrounds

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: `#FAFAF7` (warm ivory) — like quality legal paper
- Accent: `#1E3A5F` (deep navy) — legal authority
- Text: `#1C1C1E` — near-black for maximum legibility
- Emotional intent: "Clean, precise, trustworthy."

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Single-column editorial layout
- Centered hero with large serif headline
- Horizontal rule dividers between sections

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal rules as section dividers
- Small caps for section labels
- Monospaced font for contact details

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Minimal animations — only subtle fade transitions
- Underline hover effects on links

**Animation:**
- Subtle opacity fade-ins only

**Typography System:**
- Headers: Playfair Display — editorial authority
